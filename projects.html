Wireless MESH Ad-Hoc Network Wildfire Early Warning System by using applied rules based on FDRS Malaysia Standard

Advisor : En HASRULNIZAM BIN HASHIM (KKTM PJ)

Executive Summary :

Forests, one of the most valuable and necessary resources, a natural habitat to animals and forest products are vital in our lives in many direct and indirect ways. However, Wildfires can burn acres of land and destroy everything in their paths in mere minutes. Wildfire destroys homes, animals, trees and plants, wildlife as well. Due to their uncontrolled unpredictable rapid growth and behaviors, they can quickly become difficult to contain, leading to significant loss of lives, wildlife and property. The study proposes a MESH Network prototype where the Transceiver would communicate each other in receive and push data to the IoT Middleware FAVORIOT with reading scale calculated theory from Malaysia Fire Danger Rating System.


Single Hop Wireless Sensor Network Water Quality Monitoring System by using applied rules based on NWQI Malaysia Standard

Advisor : Dr MUHAMMAD SAUFY ROHMAD (SMRi UiTM)

Executive Summary :

Water Pollution nowadays can give a significant impact towards the local and nationwide in various aspect for instance health issues. The study proposes an IoT Monitoring solutions with Wireless Sensor Network as the Network communications with selected sensory to read the parameter of the water quality based on the Malaysia NWQI Standards.
